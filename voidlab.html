voidLab archive

voidLab was an intersectional feminist collective for women, non-binary, gender nonconforming, trans and queer people to express individual identities through art and technology. This archive publication documents their projects, actions, and reflections, and includes words by Dorothy R. Santos, Safiya Umoja Noble, An Xiao Mina, Aria Dean, and Nora N. Khan.
